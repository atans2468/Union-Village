Our Mission

To design, develop and build an Integrated Health Village as a model of state-of-the art technology and environmental sustainability featuring a world-class healthcare center integrated into retail, entertainment and cultural centers as well as “smart” senior and residential communities to produce 17,000 (direct, indirect and construction) jobs and a dynamic economic engine generating nearly $10 billion in tax revenues over 25 years.
