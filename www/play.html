Henderson Hospital

Henderson Hospital

The “anchor” for UnionVillage is the hospital which has broken ground and is under construction. This new hospital will be sixth Valley Health System facility in greater Las Vegas. The hospital will offer high-demand medical care, including general surgery, emergency and specialty services. The hospital plans to open for business on October 31, 2016. This hospital is owned by Universal Health Services (NYSE: UHS), the second largest healthcare provider in the United States.

Skilled Nursing and Long Term Acute Care Hospital

Skilled Nursing and Long Term Acute Care Hospital

UnionVillage is proud to announce the signing of a LOI with a major subacute provide to build a state of the art Skilled Nursing Facility and Long Term Acute Care Hospital.

Franklin Park Senior Living

Franklin Park Senior Living

UnionVillage is also proud to announce the signing of Joint Venture Agreement with Franklin Senior Living of Austin, Texas. Franklin is the industry leader in the next big thing in Senior Living, the Wellness Village. The focus on the Wellness Village is education and the pursuit of wellness. Residents will have access to not only the finest healthcare in the State, but a state-of-the-art wellness center which will incorporate; fitness, nutrition, rehab, retail pharmacy, dental clinic, with Western and Eastern Medicine.

The Senior Living component of UnionVillage is scheduled to be three phases of 300 units, which will house over 1,400 seniors. The plan is to provide all three levels of senior living which are; Independent Living, Assisted Living and Memory Care Living.

Wolff Townhome Apartments

Wolff Townhome Apartments

UnionVillage has recently signed a Purchase and Sale Agreement with The Wolff Company,. Founded in 1949, The Wolff Company is one of the leading investor and developer of Apartments in the US and is the largest apartment owner in Las Vegas. The Wolff Company is in escrow for 14 acres of land and is planning on immediately building 300 high end townhome/apartments. Construction should begin early 2016.

Wellness Center

Wellness Center

UnionVillage has signed a Letter of Intent with UHS to lease back 50% of the projected space in a 40,000 SF Wellness Center at UnionVillage. The Company also has a commitment of funding from Inland EB5 company, and projects construction to commence in early 2016.

Dialysis Clinic

Dialysis Clinic

UnionVillage has signed a Letter of Intent with a group of physicians associated with Dr. Larry Lehrner and the Kidney Specialist of Southern Nevada, the largest Nephrology group is Nevada. The group intends to buy 1 acre of land and construct a 10,000 SF dialysis clinic adjacent to the Assisted Living Community and Skilled Nursing Facility. The building is expect to be under construction in 2016.

Las Vegas Athletic Club

Las Vegas Athletic Club

UnionVillage has completed the sale of 10 acres to the Las Vegas Athletic Club who will soon be under construction on a 95,000 SF state-of-the-art Athletic Club. LVAC is the largest athletic club with seven (7) facilities spread throughout the great Las Vegas Area.

Medical Office Building

Medical Office Building

UHS will soon start construction on the first MOB at UnionVillage. The building is expected to be a three story building and occupy 80,000 SF. UHS has plans to build a second Medical Building in the near future which also will be 120,000 SF
