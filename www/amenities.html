UnionCentre

UnionCentre

UnionCentre – a State-of-the-Art and World-Class Healthcare Center comprised, in its initial phase, a full continuum of care including an Acute Care Hospital, an Ambulatory Care Center, a Long Term Acute Care Hospital, a Skilled Nursing Facility, Medical Office Buildings and a Wellness Center to serve the people of South Nevada as a regional, national and international center of excellence for decades. The first phase is anticipated to be 450,000 Square Feet and would include 307 beds.

Acute Care Hospital – Valley Health Systems “VHS” http://www.valleyhealthsystemlv.com will be building a 200+ bed hospital at UnionVillage, which has been named Henderson Hospital. VHS has six other hospitals in Nevada and five others in Clark County. The majority owner of VHS is Universal Health Services (NYSE: UHS) the second largest for profit health system in the United States. The hospital is scheduled to open in the 4th quarter of 2016

Long Term Acute Care Hospital “LTACH” – UnionVillage has signed a Letter of Intent with a large provider who will open a 45 bed LTACH in the 3rd Quarter of 2016

Skilled Nursing Facility “SNF” – UnionVillage has signed a Letter of Intent with a provider who will open a 120 bed SNF in the 3rd Quarter of 2016

Ambulatory Care PAVILION “ACP” –In the 4th Quarter of 2016 VHS will open a 50,000 SF Ambulatory Care Pavilion which will host a number of health and wellness related uses including; Surgi-Center, Wound Care, Pharmacy, Physical Therapy, etc.

Medical Office Buildings “MOB’s” – VHS will open at least one 100,000 SF MOB in the 4th Quarter of 2014

Wellness Mall – UnionVillage is developing a 50,000 SF Wellness Mall which will focus on the seven components of wellness: Social, Spiritual, Physical, Occupational, Intellectual, Emotional, and Environmental. The Wellness Mall will include a Wellness orientated Health Club, a Spa, and all types of Eastern and Western Wellness Clinics.

UnionPlace

UnionPlace

UnionPlace – UnionPlace is a Wellness Retirement Village of the future for up to 1,500 vibrant seniors, as well as those with special service needs for assisted living and memory care. Included in UnionPlace will be every resource to remain “connected” to families and healthcare professionals and to continue an unequaled quality of life and personal growth: video conferencing; virtual university and learning center; virtual competition in golf, bowling, tennis and other activities; virtual travel throughout the world; and, a virtual Center for Arts & Entertainment.

The World’s First Wellness Village: The Wellness Village is a new concept developed by UnionVillage. The Wellness Village is the first paradigm shift in Senior Living in decades. In addition to traditional services (apartments, meals and housekeeping, and activities), the Wellness Village coordinates education, and Healthcare and Wellness for the Villagers. The Wellness Village empowers the resident to grow in all aspects of their lives, and helps focus Villagers on living “longer, healthier and happier lives.”

1st Phase: UnionVillage is currently looking for Senior Living Partners who will provide the management services for the Village. The first phase is scheduled to be 300 units and over 500,000 SF

Juliet Companies – The co-developer for the retail is the Juliet Companies http://julietcompanies.com , a well know local developer in the Las Vegas Area.

Athletic Club – The Las Vegas Athletic Club (http://www.lvac.com ) will be the retail anchor on the northern most area of the Plaza. LVAC is well known in the Las Vegas area featuring the most number of facilities and the largest number of members.
